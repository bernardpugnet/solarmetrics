Embeddable Solar Widgets

Integrate Solar Data Atlas widgets for free on your website. Updated data, responsive and easy to implement.

How to use?

  1. 1. Select the widget you're interested in
  2. 2. Click "Copy integration code"
  3. 3. Paste the code into your HTML page
  4. 4. The widget will display automatically

Available Widgets

Display the levelized cost of solar electricity (LCOE) in real-time, compared to competing sources.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the widgets free? ▼

Yes, all our widgets are 100% free and unlimited. No API key required.

Can I customize the widget style? ▼

Widgets are optimized to adapt to your design via their dimensions. For advanced customization, contact us.

Is widget data updated in real-time? ▼

Yes, data (LCOE, rates, productivity) is updated daily or at each widget load.

Can I use multiple widgets on the same page? ▼

Of course! You can combine as many widgets as you want on a single page.
