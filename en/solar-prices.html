7 Trends & Outlook 2026

Asian Overproduction

Global module oversupply (650 GW vs 600 GW demanded) pressuring prices downward, especially Chinese PERC and TOPCon modules.

Rising EU Industry

European production capacities (France, Germany, Spain) increasing thanks to Net-Zero Industry Act and EIF support.

LFP Batteries: Continuous Decline

LFP costs falling (projections 500-600 €/kWh 2027) due to competition (BYD, CATL, EV Cells). Residential storage profitability improving.

Massive Rollout Expected

BNEF forecasts 649 GW solar deployment in 2026, driven by SDGs and competitive buyback tariffs.

Last updated: March 2026
