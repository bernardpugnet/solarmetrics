3 European regulatory framework

🇪🇺 RED III - Renewable Energy Directive

Revised directive (2023) setting binding targets for member states:

42.5% renewables in EU energy mix by 2030

Binding target, raised from 32% to 42.5%

Permitting acceleration

Max delays: 12 months (acceleration zones) to 24 months

Overriding public interest

Renewables presumed of major public interest

🌱 EU Taxonomy

Classification of sustainable economic activities to guide financial flows :

Solar PV = Green activity

Eligible and aligned with the taxonomy

Technical criteria

Substantial contribution + Do No Significant Harm (DNSH)

Financing impact

Easier access to green bonds and green loans

Corporate PPA boom

15 GW

PPAs signed in Europe in 2024

+25%

Annual growth

10-15 yrs

Average contract duration

Large corporates (tech, industry, retail) secure their green electricity supply through long-term PPAs, reducing their exposure to market prices and scope 2 emissions.
