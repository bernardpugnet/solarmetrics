⚠️ Assumptions & limitations of this simulation

Irradiation data — sourced from PVGIS (European Commission), TMY database. Reflects a typical meteorological year, not actual year-to-year variability.

Tariffs & subsidies — pre-filled from public sources (Eurostat, national regulators). Indicative values that may be outdated. Check your electricity bill and installer quotes.

Taxation — not integrated or only partially. Actual tax impact (income tax on feed-in revenue, VAT, capital gains) depends on your personal situation.

Maintenance — not included. Allow approximately 1–2% of initial cost per year for routine maintenance (cleaning, inverter, inspections).

Battery — simplified model (fixed efficiency, linear degradation). Does not simulate thermal management, partial cycles, or calendar ageing.

Net gains over 25 years — cumulative, not discounted. Inflation and opportunity cost of capital are not factored in.

Sensitivity — results are sensitive to electricity price, installation cost, and consumption assumptions. A ±10% variation in these parameters can significantly change the verdict.
