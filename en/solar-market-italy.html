FAQ

What is the cost of a solar installation in Italy?
CAPEX ranges from 650-1500 €/kWp depending on the segment (utility-scale, commercial, residential). Costs are influenced by project size, local regulations and grid connection conditions.
What is the size of the solar market in Italy?
Italy has 33 GW of installed photovoltaic capacity, making it one of the main European markets.
How is the solar market evolving in Italy?
The market is growing strongly with +5.6 GW en 2025. Support policies, the increasing competitiveness of solar and European climate targets are driving investments.
