Solar Market Germany | Data & Benchmarks 2026

Germany is one of the major solar markets in Europe with 82 GW of installed photovoltaic capacity. Recent growth (+14 GW in 2025) confirms the market's dynamism. This page gathers the main economic indicators of solar energy in Germany.

Key indicators

Indicator Value
CAPEX (range) 700-1800 €/kWp
LCOE (range) 35-130 €/MWh
Installed capacity 82 GW
Recent growth +14 GW in 2025
PPA price (utility) 55-85 €/MWh

Last updated : Mars 2026.

FAQ

What is the cost of a solar installation in Germany?
CAPEX ranges from 700-1800 €/kWp depending on the segment (utility-scale, commercial, residential). Costs are influenced by project size, local regulations and grid connection conditions.
What is the size of the solar market in Germany?
Germany has 82 GW of installed photovoltaic capacity, making it one of the main European markets.
How is the solar market evolving in Germany?
The market is growing strongly with +14 GW in 2025. Support policies, the increasing competitiveness of solar and European climate targets are driving investments.
