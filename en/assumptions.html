Limitations & Notes

  • • Module prices are FOB (Free On Board) prices, excluding transport and European import taxes.
  • • Residential installed costs include labor, Consuel connection and commissioning.
  • • FIT tariffs change quarterly — check photovoltaique.info.
  • • Self-consumption bonus may be modified by ministerial decree.
  • • CO₂ factors vary by time of day (merit order).
  • • Battery prices evolve rapidly (-15-20%/year) — data to be updated.

Methodology

Compilation of verified public sources. All prices are in current euros unless noted otherwise. Ranges reflect geographic and technological dispersion. Each parameter is documented with its source, enabling full reproducibility of calculations. The dataset is versioned and dated to ensure traceability of assumptions used in our analyses. Quarterly updates are planned to reflect market evolution.
